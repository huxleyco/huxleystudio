Who we are

Huxley & Co. ("we," "us," or "our") is a boutique executive search and private advisory firm. Our principal place of business is 9171 Wilshire Blvd, Suite 500, Beverly Hills, CA 90210. We can be reached at hello@thehuxleycompany.com.

Information we collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us through our website contact form, including:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Organization name
  • Nature of inquiry and any brief you choose to share

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. We do not run analytics software that collects personal data without your knowledge.

How we use your information

Information you submit is used solely to respond to your inquiry and, where relevant, to conduct the search or advisory engagement you have requested. We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third party, except as required by law or as strictly necessary to fulfill an engagement (for example, with a candidate you have asked us to evaluate).

All inquiries and engagement details are treated with absolute discretion. This is not a legal obligation we discharge reluctantly — it is fundamental to how we operate.

Data retention

We retain your contact information and correspondence for as long as is necessary to manage our relationship with you, or as required by applicable law. If you would like us to delete your information, please contact us at hello@thehuxleycompany.com and we will do so promptly.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you, or to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly. We will respond within 30 days.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Contact

For any questions regarding this policy or how we handle your data, please write to us at hello@thehuxleycompany.com or at our Beverly Hills address above.
