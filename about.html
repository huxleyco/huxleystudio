Laura

FOUNDER & PRINCIPAL

Laura Huxley built her practice at the intersection of talent and trust. With a background spanning some of the world's largest and most high-profile productions, venture capital, and UHNW private client advisory, she founded Huxley & Co. to bring that same rigor to the people side of exceptional organizations. Her work spans design, architecture, and the creative industries, with a particular focus on family offices and private clients who require both discretion and depth. A career spent across both sides of the Atlantic has shaped an advisory practice that understands how exceptional people operate, wherever they are in the world.

PRINCIPAL, ADVISORY & STRATEGY

Chad Siwik brings to Huxley & Co. a rare fluency in high-stakes environments and complex institutional relationships. As a Program Manager on NASA's Artemis mission, he works directly with astronauts and senior government figures, navigating the demands of high-level talent relations within one of the most security-conscious organizations in the world. His expertise in strategic messaging and narrative development across multiple audiences has been shaped by years of operating where precision, discretion, and trust are not optional.
