The Team

Principal Advisors

Founder

Laura Huxley

Founder & Principal

Laura Huxley built her practice at the intersection of talent and trust. With a background spanning some of the world's largest and most high-profile productions, venture capital, and private client advisory, she founded Huxley & Co. to bring that same rigor to the people side of exceptional organizations. Her work spans design, architecture, and the creative industries, with a particular focus on family offices and private clients who require both discretion and depth. A career spent across both sides of the Atlantic has shaped an advisory practice that understands how exceptional people operate, wherever they are in the world.

Principal

Chad Siwik

Principal, Advisory & Strategy

Chad Siwik brings to Huxley & Co. a rare understanding of what it means to operate where the stakes are real and discretion is non-negotiable. As a Program Manager on NASA's Artemis mission, he works directly with astronauts and senior government figures, navigating talent relations, institutional trust, and high-stakes communication within one of the most security-conscious organizations in the world. That experience translates directly into how he approaches advisory work: with precision, care, and an instinct for what matters most to the people in the room.
