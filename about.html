BACKGROUND

Laura Huxley founded Huxley & Co. after a career spanning talent advisory across four distinct industries, each defined by its own demands, discretion requirements, and calibre of principal.

Her work has taken her from the operational complexity of large-scale global events to the nuanced world of venture capital, where she has advised on the leadership structures that define a firm's culture and trajectory. In the family office and private individual space, she brings a depth of understanding that only comes from years of trusted, confidential work with UHNW clients.

Her background in design and architecture has shaped a precise aesthetic sensibility and an understanding of the creative industries that informs how she identifies and places exceptional talent.

Outside of work, Laura is a private pilot who travels widely and is dedicated to healthy living and her family.
