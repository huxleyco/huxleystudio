The Process

I

Introductory Conversation

Every engagement begins with a conversation, not a questionnaire. We take the time to understand not just the role, but the organization, the culture, and the standard you hold yourself to.

II

Principal-Led Search

We conduct every search personally. No junior associates. No delegated sourcing. We draw on our global network, cultivated across decades of trust-based relationships.

Curated Presentation

We present a small number of candidates, never a longlist designed to demonstrate effort. Each name we put forward, we would stake our own reputation on.
