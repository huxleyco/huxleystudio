How We Work

The Process

I

Introductory Conversation

Every engagement begins with a conversation, not a questionnaire. We take the time to understand not just the role, but the organization, the culture, and the standard you hold yourself to. We will ask questions that go beyond the brief. That is intentional.

II

Principal-Led Search

We conduct every search personally. No junior associates. No delegated sourcing. We draw on our global network, cultivated across decades of trust-based relationships.

III

Curated Presentation

We present a small number of candidates, never a longlist designed to demonstrate effort. Each has been interviewed in depth, thoroughly referenced, and considered against your specific brief. We would stake our own reputation on every name we put forward.
