How We Work

The Process

I

Introductory Conversation

Every engagement begins with a conversation, not a questionnaire. We take the time to understand not just the role, but the organisation, the culture, and the standard you hold yourself to. We will ask questions that go beyond the brief. That is intentional.

II

Principal-Led Search

We conduct every search personally. No junior associates. No delegated sourcing. We draw on a private network built across London and Los Angeles over many years | reaching candidates who are not looking, and who would not respond to anyone else.

III

Curated Presentation

We present a small number of candidates, never a longlist designed to demonstrate effort. Each has been interviewed in depth, thoroughly referenced, and considered against your specific brief. We would stake our own reputation on every name we put forward.

The Team

Founder

Laura Huxley

Laura grew up in Bristol and has spent her career at the intersection of exceptional work and the people who make it. She began in London in PR across art, music, events, and television, before moving into talent advisory in Los Angeles and Europe. For four years she was embedded within one of the world's leading multidisciplinary creative studios, advising on the people behind some of the largest productions globally.

She also works with venture capital firms, family offices, and founders who hold themselves to an uncommon standard. A student of psychology and a trainee pilot, she founded Huxley & Co. on the belief that understanding people, how they think, what they need, and where they thrive, is the only honest basis for placing them.
